I am a mechanical engineer with an MSc in Computer Graphics, Games and Virtual Reality. My academic projects focus on rendering, simulation, real-time systems and interactive experiences.

My path

From mechanical engineering to computer graphics.

My background in mechanical engineering shaped the way I approach computer graphics: through structure, mathematics and physical reasoning.

After graduating from Politecnico di Milano, I specialised in Computer Graphics, Games and Virtual Reality at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, where I developed projects involving rendering, GPU programming, simulation, Unity and immersive interaction.

I am especially interested in the point where technical systems and visual design meet. I enjoy understanding how things work, implementing them from the ground up and presenting the result in a clear and engaging way.

Illustration, drawing and digital art remain an important part of my practice and influence how I approach interfaces, scenes, composition and visual communication.
